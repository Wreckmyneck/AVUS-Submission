About AVUS

Welcome to AVUS, the AI-generated vs Human-written Detection Software.

Development and Purpose

AVUS was developed as part of the MSc Dissertation project at Queen's University Belfast (QUB) by Conor Buick under the guidance of Dr. Reza Rafiee. The project aimed to classify text as either AI generated or human written.

Indicators and Models

To achieve this task, four effective indicators and models were discovered:

Perplexity readings of sentences
Term-frequency-inverse-document-frequency of n-grams from the text
Term-frequency-inverse-document-frequency of the text
Burstiness of sentences in the text

Performance

The system boasts an accuracy score of 0.97 and an F1 score of 0.96 on text data related to the same topic-fields as the training data.

Training Data

The training data encompassed a variety of sources including scientific abstracts, news articles (including CNN), short stories, internet comments (e.g., blogger and reddit), and reviews (particularly hotel reviews).

Functionality

AVUS can process raw text input, text files, PDF files, and DOCX files. Additionally, it offers the ability to generate text from a GPT model provided the user has an API key.
