Understanding Text Classification

Welcome to the Burstiness Text Classification Explanation page. This section will guide you through the intricate process that our AI model undertakes to classify text, determining whether it originates from an AI source or a human writer.

Text processing

The text is sent to be processed where it is split into individual sentences, each sentence has it's words counted and put into a frequency distrubution before they are flattened into singular features that can be analysed by an algorithm

Support Vector Machines (SVMs)

For this task a support vector machines (SVMs) model was used. SVMs are a powerful tool in the world of machine learning, designed to tackle classification and regression tasks. SVMs excel at handling complex relationships within data, making them a popular choice for a wide range of applications.
